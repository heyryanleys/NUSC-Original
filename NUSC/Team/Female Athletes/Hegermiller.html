2014-2015 Northeastern University Swim Club
Emma Hegermiller
Stroke: Backstroke
Height: 5'8"
Graduation Year: 2017
High School: Sacred Heart Academy
Hometown: Wading River, NY

Emma joined the Huskies following a successful season with her high school swim team at Sacred Heart, where she was named captain her senior year. As a backstroke specialist, Emma helped to lead her team to great successes, and looks to do the same thing for the Huskies. Outside of the pool, Emma was very involved at her high school. She was a three year member of the Outdoor Track team (2009-2011), Treasurer of the Catholic League (which she was a part of from 2009-2013), and a member of the Math Team (2009-2013). As a smart, competitive and dedicated individual, Emma is a great example of what you can achieve with hard work and dedication.
